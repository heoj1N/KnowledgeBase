The rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) has significantly impacted Project Management (PM), offering new possibilities for automation, assistance, and prediction of project outcomes. This paper explores the development of AI technology over recent years and its applications in project management.

## Key Findings

- Accelerated development of machine learning (ML) technologies towards deep learning (DL)
- Natural language processing (NLP) and computer vision (CV) applications in PM
- Current state of AI applications in the construction industry
- Future potentials and application areas of AI in PM

## Impact on Project Management

The integration of AI in project management has led to:
- Enhanced automation of routine tasks
- Improved decision-making through predictive analytics
- Better resource allocation and optimization
- Risk assessment and mitigation

## Future Directions

The research indicates that while AI applications in PM are still in their early stages, there is significant potential for growth and innovation. The paper provides a foundation for future research and practical applications in this evolving field.
